Background on Zapier
--------------------

Zapier is **an online automation tool that connects your apps and services**. You can connect two or more apps to automate repetitive tasks without coding or relying on developers to build the integration.

![](/images/google1.jpeg)
Fully Automating New Member Information
---------------------------------------

Step 1: Connect to Zapier
-------------------------

**Under "People", navigate to "Members" > "Invite Members".**

![](/images/google2.png)

**Click "Connect to Zapier".**

![](/images/google3.png)

**Sign Into Zapier or Create a New Account.**

![](/images/google4.png)

Step 2: Create a Zap
--------------------

**Click "Create".**

![](/images/google5.png)

**Click "Zaps".**

![](/images/google6.png)

**Type this Text Word for Word.**  
_"When a new member is uploaded in Google Sheets, reflect the change in IntrosAI"_
![](/images/google7.png)

**Click the "+" for Both Suggested Actions.**

![](/images/google8.png)

**The Two Actions Will Appear on the Main Dashboard.**

![](/images/google9.png)

**Choose an Account (Your Google Sheets Account)**

![](/images/google10.png)

**Choose Your Spreadsheet & Worksheet**

![](/images/google11.png)

**Test the Trigger**

![](/images/google12.png)

**Confirm "Create Member" Event**

![](/images/google13.png)

**Choose Your IntrosAI Account**

![](/images/google14.png)

**Fill in First Name, Last Name, & Email**

![](/images/google15.png)

**Test Step**

![](/images/google16.png)

Step 3: Check
-------------

**Insert the Test Member's First Name, Last Name, and Email**

![](/images/google17.png)

<Tip>
    **Pro Tip:** You can upload multiple at once. Let's say you copy 100 rows, IntrosAI will reflect the update. This can save you bundles of time. 

![](/images/google18.png)

![](/images/google19.png)

</Tip>

**Check in IntrosAI under "People" > "Members" > "Uploaded"**

![](/images/google20.png)

Recap
-----

You have learned how to connect your Zapier account, create your Zap that adds a member directly from Google Sheets into your IntrosAI account, and check the results!
