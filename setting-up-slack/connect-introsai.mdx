## Connecting to Slack

**(Step 1/3): Navigate to "Customize & Insights" > "Channels".**

![](/images/introsai-slack1.png)
**(Step 2/3): Click "Slack".**

![](/images/introsai-slack2.png)

<Note>
**Note:** You have 1 of 2 options: Email or Slack. Not both.
</Note>

**(Step 3/3): Connect Your Slack.**

![](/images/introsai-slack3.png)

Recap
-----

You've learned how to connect your slack account by navigating to channels and connecting your slack account.
